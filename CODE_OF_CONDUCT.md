# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

bcp-sdk is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive open source community. Contributors, reviewers, maintainers, and users are expected to collaborate in good faith and treat others with professionalism.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.
- Show empathy toward other community members.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, reviews, or releases.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any communication channel maintained by the project — and to anyone representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Reports of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be sent to **conduct@pointeight.ai**. Reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers may remove comments, commits, code, issues, pull requests, or contributors that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from common open source community standards.
